Green Environment Beauty Contest

The website allows participants to upload 15-second videos and images related to the green environment. The uploaded videos and images are displayed side by side, and visitors to the website can vote for their favorite video or image. The winner of the contest receives a prize. The website an aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly experience. The website also promotes environmental conservation and sustainability by including educational content about the green environment. Overall, the website provides a platform for individuals to showcase their creativity while promoting the importance of environmental protection.
